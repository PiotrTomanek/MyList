What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

No frameworks, no bloat, no subscription nags. The core app is a single file that runs anywhere — your tasks never leave your device unless you export them.

✦ Carousel categories

Swipe between your worlds — Work, Home, Side Project. The URGENT view automatically pulls in every urgent task across all lists. Your daily hit list.

✦ Priority system

URGENT, HIGH, MEDIUM, or relax — it's on the list. Mark what matters, ignore the rest.

✦ Steps & subtasks

Break big tasks into numbered steps without drowning in nested hierarchies.

✦ Swipe to delete

Satisfying, fast, clean. Done means gone.

✦ Your data, exported

JSON for backups, TXT for humans, .ics for Apple & Google Calendar. Your data stays yours.

✦ Works offline

Everything runs locally in your browser. No account, no signal, no problem.

Philosophy

Most task apps want to be platforms.

They want your email, your subscription, your attention, your data. MyList wants almost nothing. The core is still just a file. The only thing that ever touches a server is photo import — and only when you choose to use it.
